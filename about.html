How to use Senator Stock Watch

Senator Stock Watch is a webpage that displays data about recent stock transactions from senators who actively participate in stock trading.

  • Insider Trading Prevention: Investors can track stock market trades of senators in a convenient way.
  • Fast delivery: New trades and analytics are published as close to real-time as possible, providing the most up-to-date STOCK Act information source available.
  • Trade reporting: Curated trading data from the last four years can be easily accessed with details such as date, politician, stock, political party and more.
